Project Overview

AarkVoid proposes the development of a modern, secure, and scalable Purchase Order Management designed specifically for GSLF and FGSPL.

The solution will streamline the complete purchase order lifecycle from vendor management and PO creation to GST calculation, approvals, PDF generation, reporting, and deployment through an intuitive web application capable of native deployment across desktop and mobile form factors.
